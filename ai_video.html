AI Video Making

Harness the power of Sora, Runway, Pika, and more to produce stunning AI-driven videos.

Why AI Video Making?

AI-powered video tools revolutionize content creation—automating editing, generating effects, and enabling rapid prototyping without heavy hardware or extensive editing skills.

Key Platforms & Their Strengths

RunwayML

Real-time video editing with machine learning models—green screen removal, style transfer, and more via an intuitive UI.

Sora

Text-to-video generation: convert scripts into storyboard animations with customizable characters and environments.

Pika Labs

AI-assisted video synthesis: generate loopable animations, apply stylized effects, and refine frames interactively.

Step-by-Step Tutorials

  • Getting started with RunwayML: importing clips, selecting models, exporting outputs.
  • Crafting scripts in Sora: best practices for prompt structure and scene descriptions.
  • Enhancing animations in Pika: using keyframes, loop settings, and style presets.

Best Practices

  1. Script clarity: write concise prompts or storyboards to guide the AI accurately.
  2. Resolution & format: choose output dimensions that match your platform (YouTube, Instagram, etc.).
  3. Post-production: refine AI-generated clips with traditional editing tools for final polish.
  4. Iteration: experiment with parameters (frame rate, style strength) to find the right aesthetic.
