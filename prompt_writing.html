Why Prompt Writing Matters

Effective prompts are the key to unlocking the full potential of AI tools. Whether you're generating text with ChatGPT, images with Midjourney, or code with Copilot, a well-structured prompt guides the model to deliver accurate, creative, and relevant outputs.

Anatomy of a Great Prompt

  • Context: Provide background or scenario.
  • Instruction: Clearly state the task.
  • Constraints: Specify style, length, format.
  • Examples: Show examples or templates.

Prompt Templates & Examples

"Write a 300-word short story in the style of Edgar Allan Poe about a haunted lighthouse. Include vivid sensory details and a twist ending."
